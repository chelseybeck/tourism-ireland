Major Regions of Ireland

Dublin

Best time to visit: June to August. Dublin offers a mix of historic sites, vibrant culture, and lively nightlife.

Galway

Best time to visit: April to October. Known for its festivals, traditional music, and beautiful coastal scenery.

Kerry

Best time to visit: May to September. Famous for the Ring of Kerry, with stunning landscapes and outdoor activities.

Connemara

Best time to visit: March to May. Offers rugged landscapes, serene lakes, and a peaceful atmosphere.

Clare

Best time to visit: April to June. Home to the Cliffs of Moher and a rich traditional music scene.

Antrim

Best time to visit: May to September. Known for Giant's Causeway and stunning coastal views.
